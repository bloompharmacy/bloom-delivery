The Problem

Every Day, the Same Questions Cost Us Time

Across the operation, the same questions come up — over and over. Without a system, every answer takes phone calls, guesswork, and lost time.

"Where is the driver right now?"
No live visibility — staff calls drivers to find out.
"Did the order arrive?"
No way to confirm delivery without contacting the driver.
"What's this order's value?"
Cashier can't verify totals when settling with a driver.
"Which zone is the driver in?"
Drivers cover overlapping or unclear areas.
"What happened with order #X?"
Old orders and disputes have no searchable history.
"How much do we pay this driver?"
End-of-month shipping costs calculated manually — error-prone.

Every unanswered question = lost customers, wasted effort, and money slipping away.
